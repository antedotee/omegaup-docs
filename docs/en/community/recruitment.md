# Recruitment Process

omegaUp welcomes new volunteers to join our engineering team! This document describes the training process for new contributors.

## Training Process

The training process is open to anyone interested in contributing. No special request needed!

### Resources

1. **Documentation**: [omegaUp Wiki](https://github.com/omegaup/omegaup/wiki)
2. **Video Tutorial**: [Development Environment Setup](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=H1PG4Dvje88)
3. **Discord**: [#dev_training channel](https://discord.com/invite/K3JFd9d3wk) for technical help
4. **Good First Issues**: [Curated beginner-friendly issues](https://github.com/omegaup/omegaup/issues?q=is%3Aissue+is%3Aopen+label%3A%22Good+first+issue%22)
5. **Good Second Issues**: [Next level challenges](https://github.com/omegaup/omegaup/issues?q=is%3Aissue+is%3Aopen+label%3A%22Good+second+issue%22)

## Requirements to Join Team

To be formally invited to the engineering team:

1. ✅ Read all documentation
2. ✅ Resolve 5 issues from "Good first issues" list
3. ✅ Resolve 5 issues from "Good second issues" list

Once completed, you'll be:
- Formally invited to join the engineering team
- Offered an @omegaup.com email account (if desired)

## Exceptions

Some exceptions to this process:
- Internship students with contracts
- Former volunteers with recognized contribution records
